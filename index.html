About RANDOM ART

This website is inspired by “Dada” - art movement of the early 20th century. One of the key ideas of “Dada” was that talent, training and academic means should be eliminated from the process of making art. Many "Dada" art works were created by exploring randomness of chance. The Dadaists creed was “Chance must be recognized as a new stimulus to artistic creation.”

Here you can make your own image with random number of shapes with random color,size, position and transparency.

Go ahead, be an Artist!
